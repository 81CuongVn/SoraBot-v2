Why Use Sora?


Fully-Featured Music

Sora is jam packed with awesome music features. These range from extremely fast loading of songs to the ability to export, share and import custom playlists with ALL users of Sora! You can search and vote on already shared playlists too and make yours climb to the top!


Extensive Help

Sora probably has one of the most extensive Wikis in any Discord bot. Ever. If that is not enough for you then we also have a Discord Support Guild


All in One

Sora has nearly every feature you could wish for and thus eliminates the need of 100 Bots in your guild just to satisfy your needs. If an important feature is missing you can request it in the Support Guild and it mostly gets added within a week or two - assuming your request is reasonable and actually useful to a broad spectrum of users.


Fully Configurable

You can configure Sora however you wish on a Guild-to-Guild basis. He features easy to use permission system that works with roles or the Discord Guild Perms and thus makes it easy to give your Moderators access to some of the configurability of Sora. A neat little Webpanel is also in the works!


Live Support

If you ever get stuck and the Wiki can't help you then you can always join our support guild where we got support for at least 12h a day! There was never a problem that we couldn't solve and we plan on keeping this success rate.


Built and Maintained with Love

Sora was built with absolute love and passion and is also maintained that way. If new bugs appear they get fixed within hours to max a couple days. No stone is left unturned to make Sora the greatest he can be. Many new features are planned that will double Sora's functionality in the very near future.

Need Help?


Join our Support Guild

Join our awesome support guild if you need live chat help! Me - the creator - but also many experienced programmers that helped to create Sora are there and ready to help you with any of your problems!


Open an Issue

You can also just open an Issue on our Github/Gitlab. The dev will be notified on creation via push-notification on his phone and thus they get resolved very quickly.
